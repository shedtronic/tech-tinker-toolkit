Helpful educational technology resources for teachers, makers, and learners.

Welcome to **Tech Tinker Toolkit** a hub of practical lesson packs, creative technology worksheets, and maker-led activity guides.

Whether you’re teaching Technology, Art, Engineering or Computing, we have all kinds of fun resources such as micro:bit coding, game making, animation, sound, design thinking and all kinds of hands-on maker projects, this site will help you discover ideas that are easy to share, adapt, and print for the classroom.

****

**Resources include:**

- Lesson-ready activities, idea sheets, and workshop plans to help learners to develop curiosity and problem-solving as independent learners.
- Hands-on coding adventures, interactive wearables, and sensor challenges for classroom maker spaces.
- Creative worksheets and project guides for animation, storytelling, and effects.
- Music and audio production resources, remix ideas, and content creation.
- Simple build guides, materials lists, and reflection prompts for low-cost STEAM projects.
