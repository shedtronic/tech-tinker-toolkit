Tech Tinker Toolkit offers practical, classroom-ready resources through our **Teachers Pay Teachers store**.

**About the store**

Tech Tinker Toolkit curates ready-to-use lesson packs, worksheets, and maker activities for educators who want engaging STEAM and creative technology materials. These resources are designed to empower teachers, support growth mindset learning, and make it easy to bring creative technology projects into the classroom.

**Experience**

Tech Tinker Toolkit is built by an experienced educator and creative technologist with a background in music technology, visual arts, game design, and computer science. This interdisciplinary experience informs a unique approach to resource design, blending art, audio, coding, and design thinking into practical classroom materials.

- Music Technology: Resources that connect sound, technology, and creative audio exploration.

- Visual Arts: Materials that support visual storytelling, media arts, and expressive design.

- Game and Narrative Design: Lessons that help learners shape immersive experiences and meaningful play.

- Computer Science: Activities grounded in computing fundamentals, computational thinking, and maker innovation.

This interdisciplinary style forms the basis of a holistic teaching approach, helping learners develop curiosity, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

**Teaching style**

Tech Tinker Toolkit resources support a maker-led studio approach where students explore through play, collaboration, and project-centered design thinking. Lessons encourage creative risk-taking in a welcoming environment, with hands-on challenges that promote both individual skill building and teamwork.

**Background**

Tech Tinker Toolkit draws on experience across music, art, game design, and computer science to deliver resources that are both creative and educational. The store is built around the belief that technology learning should be accessible, engaging, and relevant for diverse learners.

**Additional information**

Tech Tinker Toolkit brings a real-world creative perspective to teaching resources, with materials shaped by music production, visual design, and interactive storytelling.
